Love, Life & Music

"It all started at a party, way back in 1990...

After a decade of 80's synth-pop and big haired, crotch grinding rock bands, I started to find that my tastes were 'maturing'. I had no idea at the time what I was really into musically and, at a student party at a friends cousins house, I heard Pearl Jam's 10 album for the very first time. So began my love for grunge! My tastes are varied these days, if its real musicians, playing real songs on real instruments then I'm in.

At the later stages of the 90's the inevitable happened and I joined a band, this sparked my love for live music. The atmosphere, the sweaty crowds, the banter, even the die hards that swear they will remove your tongue if you ever dared to slate their favourite band. All of it! Nothing can top going to see, and be part of, live music."

This months featured band

Inside Job - Brighton

This 4 piece hailing from Brighton describe themselves as "Rock with a little soul" and I thoroughly agree. Great stage presence from front man Liam Green mixed with thrashing guitar solo's by axe man Greg Talbot. A must see if you get the chance!

Highlight tracks: 'Burn', 'Come get it' and 'Try this for size'.
